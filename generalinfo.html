General Information

Objective

The goal of the game is to complete all waves successfully. Each wave, enemy barbarians will spawn in from the left side of the track and advance along the designated path. To stop their advance, you must place troops on and near the path. Be careful though, some barbarians will attack you from close range and others may shoot arrows. As you advance through the waves, you will gain coins by killing enemy barbarians and completing each level. These coins can be used on buying new troops or upgrading existing troops.

The amount of money you have will be displayed here. Press this button to start the next wave. The amount of lives you have will be displayed here.

Gameplay

To place troops on the map, simply drag a troop which you can afford from the right side of the screen onto the map. Troops you can afford will show their costs in a green font. If you want to upgrade a troop, click on a troop currently placed and look in the bottom right hand corner. There you can see what upgrades are available for the troop.
